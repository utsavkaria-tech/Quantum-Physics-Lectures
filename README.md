# Quantum-Physics-Lectures
# Public Understanding of Quantum Science and Technology

## Overview
This repository documents my lecture on quantum science and quantum information theory, delivered as part of a public scientific conference conducted by GUJCOST (Gujarat Council of Science and Technology). The lecture explored both conceptual and mathematical foundations of quantum mechanics. The lectures were also taken at Lok Vigyan Kendra, Rajkot, Gujarat. 

## Topics Covered

### Foundations of Quantum Mechanics
* Planck radiation law & Ultraviolet catastrophe
* Path integral intuition
* Schrödinger equation & Born rule

### Quantum Information
* Qubits vs classical bits
* Hadamard, Pauli-X, and CNOT gates
* State-vector evolution in Hilbert space & Entanglement geometry

### Quantum Algorithms
* BBBV theorem
* Grover's algorithm & Amplitude amplification
* Interference-based computation

### Open Quantum Systems
* Kraus representation & Trace preservation
* Amplitude damping, Phase damping, & Depolarizing channels
* Lindblad master equation

### Quantum Biology
* Photosynthetic coherence
* Radical pair mechanism
* Quantum effects in biological systems

## Key Equations

### Schrödinger Equation
$$i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\psi\rangle=\hat{H}|\psi\rangle$$

### Born Rule
$$P(a)=|\langle a|\psi\rangle|^{2}$$

### General Quantum Channel
$$\mathcal{E}(\rho)=\sum_{k}E_{k}\rho E_{k}^{\dagger}$$

### Lindblad Master Equation
$$\dot{\rho}=-\frac{i}{\hbar}[H,\rho]+\sum_{k}\left(L_{k}\rho L_{k}^{\dagger}-\frac{1}{2}\{L_{k}^{\dagger}L_{k},\rho\}\right)$$

Quantum mechanics is simultaneously one of the most successful and most counterintuitive theories ever developed. This lecture aimed to bridge public intuition with modern quantum information science while preserving mathematical rigor.
